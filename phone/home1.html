  • What?

    LIFT is the ideal training program for up-and-coming leaders like yourself. It will help guide you through a term in leadership, whether that be in the Chapter, Lodge, or even a higher level of leadership.

     It is our job to help give you the resources you need to get started, execute your vision, and finish well over the course of your leadership term.

  • Who?

    Participants have been chosen for this training because your Lodge's leadership believes that the vision you have for our Order could become a reality.
